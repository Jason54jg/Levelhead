<p>Levelhead is built for the Hypixel server. The mod displays the Hypixel Network Level & other statistics of a player above their head,
in chat and in tab.</p>
<br>
<p>Custom Levelhead is an optional addon that allows players to customize what appears above their own head. 
<a href="https://sk1er.club/customlevelhead">Click here to learn more about Custom Levelhead</a></p>
